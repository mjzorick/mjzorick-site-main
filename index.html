About Me

I am a Software Engineer with a focus on web. I've worked on projects ranging from simple microsites to Wordpress sites to Roku and AmazonFire TV video applications. My current work involves an internal Wordpress site to create and manage newsletter templates, leveraging Angular 2 and Mustache.

I first became interested in development, web in particular, while in college. I was taking a class "Human-Induced Environmental Change" and the professor gave us the project option of either doing a presentation to the class, or creating a website. The website option was the preferring choice for me. Working on this project, I learned HTML and simple CSS and I was hooked.
